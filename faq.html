Frequently Asked Questions

How does the adoption process work?

Browse our available pets, select one, and complete the adoption inquiry form. Our team will review and contact you.

What are the requirements to adopt?

You must be at least 18 years old, provide valid identification, and have a suitable home for the pet.

Is there an adoption fee?

Yes. The fee helps cover vaccinations, medical care, food, and shelter maintenance.

Are the pets vaccinated and spayed/neutered?

Yes. All pets are health-checked, vaccinated, and spayed/neutered before adoption.

Can I return a pet if it doesn't work out?

Yes. Please contact us so we can assist responsibly.

Why should I adopt instead of buying?

Adoption saves lives, reduces overpopulation, and gives pets a second chance at a loving home.
