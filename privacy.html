PRIVACY POLICY

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and address (when you fill out forms for adoption or volunteering).
  • Pet Adoption Details: Information about the pet you want to adopt or support.
  • Website Usage Data: Such as your IP address, browser type, and pages you visit (collected automatically to improve our site).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process adoption or volunteer applications.
  • Communicate with you about pets, events, or updates.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Keep records for safety and legal purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with other companies for marketing.

3. How We Protect Your Data

We use secure systems and follow best practices to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Only authorized team members can view or handle your personal information.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your experience and understand how visitors use the site. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the site may not work properly.

5. Sharing of Information

We may share information only when necessary:

  • With local shelters or partner organizations for adoption processing.
  • When required by law or government authorities.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for data use anytime by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
